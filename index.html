OUR SERVICES

General Transport/Dry Van Freight

This includes products such as textiles, furniture, papers, plastics, electronics, metals

Partial Transport/LTL

This includes any type of dry or frozen shipment, that the space of a full van is not required. Substantially reducing the costs, since only the required space is contracted.

Refrigerated Freight

Load that requires being in a specified temperature such as; meat, seafood, vegetables, fruits, etc.

Drayage

It is cargo focused on container transfer from one place to another.

Flatbed Transport

Most of this cargo is cargo that exceeds the weight that a truck can contain with a regular container or also oversized loads that will not fit on a regular trailer.

Project Cargo

They are extremely heavy loads or large or very high value. For this reason it requires more time to coordinate and only handles it expert and experienced personnel in this sector.
